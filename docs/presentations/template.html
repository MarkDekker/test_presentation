What this template offers

  • Single-file HTML with inline styling and navigation.
  • Keyboard, click, and touch controls.

How to use it

  1. Copy this file and rename it.
  2. Update the title in the data-title attribute.
  3. Replace slide content with your own.

Slide Layout Helpers

Reusable Classes

  • .grid-two for two-column layouts.
  • .card and .card.highlight for emphasis.
  • .pill for compact labels.

Design Tips

  • Use short headlines; keep bullets concise.
  • Mix muted and accent colors for hierarchy.
  • Keep slides focused on one concept.

Duplicate & Share

After customizing, link your new file from the GitHub Pages index. The navigation supports:

  • Keyboard: ← / →, Space, or Shift+Space
  • Click: click the left or right half of the slide
  • Touch: swipe left or right on mobile

To keep everything self-contained, embed images as base64 or host them alongside the file within the repository.
